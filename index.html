About us
CG Kidney Care and Dialysis Centre was incorporated on November 25,2013 by Dr.Yeswanth Gangaiah.
CG Kidney Care and Dialysis Centre has emerged as the largest provider of dialysis services in Tumkur district and as the leader of stand alone dialysis services in this district. It also provides acute dialysis services for local hospitals focusing on patients with end stage renal disease.

Our Services
The staff of CG Kidney care are committed to providing the highest standard of care for our patients in the management and prevention of kidney diseases.

Patients will receive the finest health care possible delivered with dignity and respect in recognition of our patients special requirements.

We are medical professionals who will provide a quality working environment and support to enable our patients to maintain a healthy and productive life style.

We can help you with your following nephrology needs,
  • Chronic kidney disease
  • Acute kidney injury
  • Diabetes management
  • Anemia management
  • Hypertension management
  • Electrolyte imbalances

Nephrology services

  • Hemodialysis
  • Dialysis access placement
  • Renal biopsy

Hospital services

  • Acute in patient dialysis services for hospitals.
  • Dialysis patient discharge coordination.
